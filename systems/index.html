The planning → execution → review cycle

Most effective productivity systems follow a simple loop:

  • Planning — defining priorities and outcomes
  • Execution — completing focused work blocks
  • Review — adjusting systems and constraints

This loop prevents reactive work patterns and creates a sustainable workflow over time.
