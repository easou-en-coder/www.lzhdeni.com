Why these tools exist

Guides explain the idea. Tools help you do it.

Every tool here is paired with a guide on this site. The guide explains the thinking — why deep work needs protected blocks, why meetings quietly drain a team, why goals fail without a cascade — and the tool gives you a simple way to put that thinking into practice right away.

They are deliberately simple and single-purpose. Nothing here tries to replace your task manager or calendar; each tool does one useful job well, runs entirely in your browser, and keeps your data on your own device.
